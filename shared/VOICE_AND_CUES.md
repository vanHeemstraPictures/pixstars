# Voice and Cues

## Core principle

The Pixstars lamp has two complementary modes:

### 1. Deterministic mode
Ardour plays pre-rendered lines.
Use this for:
- music sync
- emotional timing
- reliability

### 2. Interactive mode
HiveMind generates or selects responses.
Use this for:
- spontaneity
- audience interaction
- experimentation

## Best practice

Keep the same lamp-state vocabulary across both modes:

- idle
- listening
- thinking
- speaking
- error
