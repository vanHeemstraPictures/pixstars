# Knowledge - Team Rockstars

This folder is the lamp's RAG knowledge base about Team Rockstars, the
commissioning party for Pixstars. The lamp considers Team Rockstars its
friends (see ai/memory/lamp_memory.yaml). The audience for the October
2026 performance is largely a Team Rockstars audience, so the lamp needs
just enough context to recognise the room.

## What goes here

Plain markdown files, one topic per file. Examples (create as needed):

- about.md           - short description of Team Rockstars (who they are,
                       what they do), suitable for the lamp's voice
- ruurd_dam.md       - the commissioning party, Managing Director, one
                       paragraph
- team_rockstars_cloud.md - context on the cloud arm (Willem works there)
- values.md          - short notes on the values the lamp can echo on
                       stage without sounding like marketing

## What does not go here

- Internal HR material, client lists, financials, anything confidential.
- Marketing copy in its raw form. Translate it into the lamp's voice
  before it lands here.
- Anything that names individual employees other than the named
  collaborators of this production.

## Format

Short markdown. Straight ASCII quotes only. If in doubt, ask Willem
before adding a name.

## Tone

The lamp's relationship to Team Rockstars is warm and slightly puzzled.
Material here should help it sound that way - never corporate, never
sycophantic.
